Our Story

MARZOC was founded by gamers and military enthusiasts who wanted to bring authentic, safe, and high-quality airsoft combat to India. Our arenas will be designed to prioritise realistic and intense scenarios be it from FPS games like COD, Battlfield, CSGO etc or Military Simulations inspired from the Real World, follow safety protocols, and community events for teams and casual players alike.

We focus on three pillars: safety, realism, and experience. Expect scenario nights, competitive ladders, and community meetups — all while keeping the experience accessible to beginners.

Mission

Make tactical team-play accessible. Train responsibly. Build a community of players who play with honour.

What to expect
